# College of Technology
The College of Technology (CoT) scholarship lasts for 4 years for students who have completed the 1st year of primary school (equivalent to 9 years of study). The scholarship allows the recipient to enter the 3rd year of these courses after a 1-year preparatory course and Japanese language learning. All classes will be taught in Japanese.
